About me
My name is Tommaso Genisi and I was born in the spring of 1997 in Vicenza, a small town of the Italian northeastern countryside.
In 2020 I gradutated in Communication Design at the Polytechnic University of Milan.
During my life I was given the opportunity to travel all around the world as well as across my country, which opened me to some life-changing experiences. My university years helped me grow radically both as a creative and as a person.
Photography and storytelling represent two vital parts of my work. In my view building an effective narrative structure is a crucial step for the positive outcome of any communicative system. I ultimately try keeping my design process as simple and captivating as possible for I strongly believe that the best way of conveying a message is to utilize just a few, carefully chosen elements.
I am extremely passionate about all those things that render the living worthwhile: music, cooking, visual arts, literature and above all, friendship.
